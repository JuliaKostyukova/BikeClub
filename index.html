London Velo Cycle Club

Enjoy. Conquer new peaks. And be in great shape.

Join us and enjoy an active weekend. Every time new interesting routes. These can be mountain trails, urban bike paths or other scenic spots. We will help to learn how to ride, those who are just starting or prepare for cycling competitions.
